Visiting coaches

Olga Bylim

With her husband, dance and studio partner Sasha, Ukrainian Amateur Champion in Standard, Latin, and 10-Dance for three years. 3-time Ukrainian Professional Ballroom Dance Champion 1993-1995. Represented the Ukraine in the European and World Ballroom Dance Championships. US Rising Star Ballroom Champion and finalist in the US National Ballroom Championships. Fred Astaire National Professional Ballroom Dance and Cabaret Champion. Currently a member of the Fred Astaire National Dance Council and a Regional Area Director of Fred Astaire Dance Studios in Long Island. NY

Eddie Apolonov

Eddie is a UK Rising Star Finalist and USA Rising Star Champion. He specializes in all areas of International Latin, Standard, American Smooth, American Rhythm, as well as choreography for showcases, solos, and novelties. Eddie is choreography specialist on Open Routines and coaches top competitive couples in the country 2005 World Class Adjudicator, NDCA. 2005 National Judge, NDCA. 2001 Manhattan Dance Sport Championship – Champion. 2001 Open USA National Championship – Finalist. 2001 Canadian Open to the World- Champion. 2001 Ohio Star Ball – Finalist.

Loreta Kriksciukaityte

Her career began by winning multiple Lithuanian championships in the youth and amateur divisions in her native Lithuania. She went on to become 5 time World Champion, 6 time European Champion, and dancing as a part of the world-famous Lithuanian formation team “Zuvedra”. Loreta has ranked among the top finalists in all the major USA competitions, rising stars finalists and a top 3 winner at the most prestigious competitions in the world including the Blackpool Dance festival in the United Kingdom.
